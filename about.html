Who We Are

A & A Global Leasing Services Limited is a duly registered company in Nigeria, specializing in Logistics Provisioning, Dedicated Corporate Haulage Services and Specialized Equipment Leasing Services. We only service Multinational Companies in view of their Operating Standards, Ethics, and Corporate Governance structure.

Our team is made up of young, dynamic, and resourceful professionals with broad business knowledge to meet the challenges of the company's objectives of providing quality products and services. The company enjoys the full participation of executive directors with proven ability in management and logistics support services.

Company portfolio size include various types of heavy-duty trailers and specialized heavy duty equipment and machines.

Our Mission

To at all times deliver exceptional services in the market value at competitive cost while adding strategic values to all our stakeholders.

Our Vision

To become a Tripple A rated Logistics Service Provision Company as well as Equipment Rental of First Choice to all Multinational Producing Companies in West Africa.

Our Team

Dr. O. YOMI-SHOLOYE

MD

A 1990 graduate of Business Administration and Master's degree holder. She started her working career with PAN OCEAN OIL CORPORATION and later joined TEXACO OVERSEAS NIGERIA PETROLEUM COMPANY LIMITED (now Chevron Oil Nigeria Plc) before founding A & A Global Leasing Services Limited.

Mr. ABAYOMI OLADAPO YOMI-SHOLOYE

Executive Director

Dapo Yomi-Sholoye is a graduate of Business Administration from Babcock University, a seasoned and Certified Business administrator and Strategy Professional with years of practical experience in Haulage and logistics Management, Strategic Business Solutions, Project supervision and management, Transport and Operations Management, Health & Safety.

He was a Fleet Supervisor and business analyst at Connecticus Nigeria Limited in 2013 and Head of Operations at International Alpha Limited in 2014 before joining A&A Global Leasing Services Ltd.
