Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

At A & A Global Leasing Services Limited, we offer a full range of haulage and logistics services designed to meet the diverse needs of multinational companies. Our solutions are tailored to ensure efficient, reliable, and cost-effective transportation of goods across various destinations, adhering to the highest operating standards and ethics.

Dedicated Corporate Haulage Services

Our haulage services provide seamless transportation of goods across Nigeria. With a modern fleet of vehicles and experienced drivers, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time.

  • Oil Tankers
  • Container Trucks
  • Cement Tankers
  • Other specialized equipment at client's request

Logistics Provisioning

We provide comprehensive logistics solutions to multinational companies, ensuring efficient movement and management of goods throughout the supply chain.

  • Provision of specialized vehicles/equipment
  • Specially trained drivers
  • Fueling services where required
  • Maintenance services

Advanced Tracking Systems

We employ state-of-the-art tracking systems to ensure the safety and efficiency of our logistics operations.

  • Online Real-Time telematics system for all Fleet Assets
  • TELEMATICS coverage for Speed, Driver Behavior, Idling and Emergencies
  • Speed Limiter Devices on all Fleet Assets
  • Geo-Fencing for all Fleet Assets
  • Physical tracking with Ground Monitoring Officers stationed in every 300km ratio
