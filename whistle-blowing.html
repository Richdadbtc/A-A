About Our Whistleblowing Policy

Our whistleblowing policy is designed to protect individuals who report concerns in good faith about actual or suspected wrongdoing within the company. We guarantee:

  • Confidential handling of all reports
  • Protection against retaliation for good faith reports
  • Thorough investigation of all credible claims
  • Appropriate action based on investigation findings

For questions about our whistleblowing policy or process, please contact our Compliance Officer at info@a-aglobaleasing.com.
